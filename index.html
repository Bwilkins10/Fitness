⚡ Quick log

4-Day Muscle Program

Upper/Lower split. Each move shows a Gym option and a Home / outdoor option — pick whichever fits the day. Log your top set so you can beat it next time.

How to grow: hit the rep target on every set, then add a little weight (or reps) next session. That's progressive overload — the whole game. Rest 90s–2min on big lifts, ~60s on the rest.

Weekly layout

A simple week: Mon Upper A · Tue Lower A · Thu Upper B · Fri Lower B · plus 1–2 easy outdoor conditioning sessions (brisk walk, jog, bike, or hill sprints 20–30 min) on off days. Take at least one full rest day. Your lawn work already counts as active recovery.

Every 6–8 weeks take a lighter "deload" week (same lifts, ~60% effort) to recover.

    📋 Your meal plan

    Built around your foods — chicken, turkey, eggs & dairy, fish, with rice and pasta/bread. Three batch-prep days that each land near ~2,900 kcal / ~190g protein on a budget (roughly $6–8/day). Rotate them or mix and match. Tap a meal's "log it" to drop it into today's food log.

    Sunday batch prep (≈1 hr): cook a big pot of rice, bake a tray of chicken (6–8 portions) and one of salmon/tilapia, boil a batch of pasta, and hard-boil some eggs. Then the week is just assembly.
    🛒 One-week grocery list (~$55–70)

    Protein: 3 dozen eggs · 4 lb chicken breast · 1 lb ground turkey + turkey sausage · deli turkey · 2 bags frozen shrimp · 1 gal milk · 2 large Greek yogurt · cheese slices/string cheese
    Carbs/snacks: big bag rice · pasta · loaf of bread · bagels · tortillas · 1 can black beans · bananas · apples · berries · box of Nature Valley Peanut bars
    Veg/fats: frozen mixed veg (2 bags) · onion · olive oil · light mayo · jar peanut butter · marinara sauce · salsa
    Worth adding: tub of whey protein (cheapest protein-per-gram there is) and creatine monohydrate.

    Muscle-building rule of thumb: ~1g protein per pound of bodyweight, hit your calorie target most days, and expect ~0.25–0.5 lb gain per week. Faster than that is mostly fat. Missed a meal on a busy lawn day? A shake of milk + 2 scoops whey + peanut butter + banana is ~600 kcal / 45g protein in 60 seconds.
